TBS GRILLSHOP

TBS Grillshop is one of Denmark's absolute largest and most well-stocked shops with everything you could want for rustic cooking.

We have been active with the webshop since 2007, and the customer base is still growing, because here every barbecue enthusiast can find the equipment they need or dream of.

Longhorn BBQ Competition

As a new addition in 2023, LongHorn BBQ will also be the Danish championship in American Barbeque at the same time.

The DM in American BBQ is not an open championship, but it is the best-placed Danish Team there will be able to boast that title and not least with an invitation to the Jack Daniel’s World Championship Invitational BBQ, which is the World Championship in real American BBQ.

The Teams that participate here are only the best from the BBQ arena in the whole world, so it is not only a huge experience but also a huge opportunity to really put your Team on the BBQ map, not only as Danish Champion in American Barbeque, but also as a participant in the Jack Daniels World Championship invitational BBQ, probably one of the most fantastic experiences you can have as a Team.

Stay updated on this page and follow as more and more information about the weekend comes out.
